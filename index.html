Animation I Recommend


Amphibia is a Disney Channel animated show from creator Matt Braly. It centres around Anne Boonchuy, a girl who gets trapped in Amphibia, a world of anthropomorphic amphibians.


The Owl House is a Disney Channel animated show from creator Dana Terrance. Luz Noceda, seeking adventure in her life, finds herself in the Demon Realm, on the side of rebels resisting the will of a genocidal emperor.


Lackadaisy is a new inde adult animation from creator Tracy J Butler. Set in the 1920s in a world of anthropomorphic cats, Lackadaisy features the workers of the eponymous speakeasy trying to keep the booze flowing.


Helluva Boss is an adult animation from creator Vivziepop. Follows the Immediate Murder Professionals, an assassin business run from the lowest depths of Hell.
