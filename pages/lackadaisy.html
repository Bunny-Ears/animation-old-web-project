Story

Lackadaisy is period crime-heist short film based on the webcomic with the same name. In 1920s St. Louis, in a world populated by cats, the speakeasy Lackadaisy, hidden below the Little Daisy Cafe, is struggeling. Rumrunners Rocky, Freckle and Ivy have been sent to collect some moonshine whiskey when they are ambushed by a team from rival speakeasy Marigold. Their adventure leads them through a quarry full of explosives. Lucky all of our characters are perfectly sane. From creator Tracy J Butler and director Fable Siegel.

Characters

  • Roark "Rocky" Rickaby - Manic and excitable is the principal bootlegger for Lackadaisy. He has a passion for destruction.
  • Calvin "Freckle" McMurray - Small and quiet, Freckle is Rocky's cousin and fathful follower. Under his fur is a bubbling rage that comes out when he goes near guns.
  • Ivy Pepper - When she's not practicing sharpshooting at college, Ivy is a dancer at Lackadaisy. With her mask of sweetness, she is a perfect distraction when on alcohol runs. Her prowess with weapons and machinery comes in useful too.
